Client Project

Website for Author

Designed and implemented a practical website for a children’s book author.

Project Summary

I supported a family friend by creating a site that could market her books while still being easy for her to manage independently. I used an online website builder, added scripting where needed, and focused on clear structure, visual identity, and editability for a non-technical user.

Technical Focus

  • Web Design
  • Site Builders
  • Basic Scripting
  • Content Structure

Client Constraint

The solution had to be easy for the author to update without developer support.

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered a maintainable site tailored for a non-technical client.
  • Improved communication of the author’s brand and book content.
  • Strengthened scripting and client-facing teaching skills.
